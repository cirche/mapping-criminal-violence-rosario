From fieldwork to original event data.

This project translates my research on criminal violence in Argentina into a public-facing visual interface. It connects qualitative fieldwork, UCDP-style event coding and geospatial exploration to analyse how violence, territory and criminal governance interact in Rosario.

01

Fieldwork-informed research

The project builds on thesis research conducted in Argentina and on contextual knowledge of Rosario's criminal dynamics.

02

Original event dataset

Events are organised through date, location, dyad, type of violence, fatalities, civilian deaths and source references.

03

Criminal governance lens

The project uses spatial visualisation to support interpretation of territorial control, civilian targeting and crimilegal orders.
