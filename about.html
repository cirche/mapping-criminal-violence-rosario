Research Profile

This project is part of a broader research focus on organised violence, urban conflict and criminal governance in Latin America.

Background.

My work focuses on the intersection between organised crime, urban violence and governance, with a particular emphasis on Argentina and the city of Rosario.

Research interests.

Urban violence and spatial dynamics

Organised crime and criminal governance

Drug trafficking and transnational networks

Event data and conflict analysis
