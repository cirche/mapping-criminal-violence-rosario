Rosario Analysis

Rosario represents one of the most critical nodes of urban violence and drug trafficking in Argentina. This section contextualises the patterns shown in the dataset.

A strategic urban space.

The city plays a central role in national and transnational trafficking routes, particularly due to its position along the Paraná river corridor.

Patterns of violence.

Frequent use of firearms and drive-by shootings.

High levels of violence targeting civilians.

Fragmentation of local criminal groups.

Violence often coordinated from prison networks.

From data to interpretation.

The dataset allows identifying recurring locations, clusters of violence and temporal trends. These patterns are interpreted through qualitative research and fieldwork insights.
